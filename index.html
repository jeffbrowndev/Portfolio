ABOUT ME

It all started with the birth of my beautiful daughter Evelyn in 2015. Coding has always been a hobby but this extreme life change inspired me to turn my passion into a career and a stable future for my family. Thus my journey with web development began, alongside an endless & inexhaustable hunger for learning.

I focus mainly on front end development. Currently I work as a freelancer building websites for small businesses. I believe that great site functionality roots from practical design and a clean, easily readable asthetic. The design process is a vital step and I enjoy it very much, yet my true passion is the code. Programming often brings up issues that I'm forced to solve in creative ways. It's like engineering a complex machine with multiple moving parts and functions, but with no blueprint. It's a consistently satisfying process.
